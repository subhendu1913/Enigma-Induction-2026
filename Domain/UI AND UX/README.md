# OBJECTIVE: INTERACTIVE BRAND EXPERIENCE

Design a digital interface for a **brand or concept of your choice using Figma**. You may design a **website screen or an app screen**.

The design should represent a **realistic digital product** and clearly communicate its purpose to the user.

---

# BRAND / CONCEPT SELECTION

You are free to choose **any ONE category listed below**. Under the chosen category, you may either:

- **Design for an existing brand** (for practice purposes only)

OR

- **Create a fictional / imaginary brand**

**Available Categories (Choose Any One):**

1. Soft Drink / Coffee Brand  
2. Food Delivery / Restaurant Brand  
3. Fashion / Clothing / Sneaker / Shoe Brand  
4. Travel / Tourism Brand  
5. Music App / OTT / Streaming Platform  

---

# DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

Your design should meet the following requirements:

● Designed using **Figma**  
● At least **one complete screen** (mobile or desktop)  
● Should resemble a **real digital interface**, not a poster  

**Must include:**

- Brand name or identity  
- Clear visual hierarchy  
- Buttons or call-to-action elements  
- Images, icons, or illustrations  

**Clean layout and readability are important.**

---

# INTERACTION & MOTION (RECOMMENDED)

Adding **interaction or motion** to your design is highly encouraged, as it helps demonstrate your understanding of **user experience**.

You may use **Figma Prototype** to add:

- Button navigation  
- Hover effects  
- Screen transitions  
- Simple animations
